Locking Pro

The Iop is always ready to lock their enemies. They get a resistance bonus based on the number of enemies they have locked at the end of their turn. If a Iop has managed to keep targets in close combat, they gain destructive potential to make their enemies suffer!

Normal effects

Per adjacent enemy at the start of the turn:
- 3% Damage inflicted (1 turn)
Per adjacent enemy at end of turn:
- 15 Elemental Resistance (1 turn)
- 10 Force of Will (1 turn)
